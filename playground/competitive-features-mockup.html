Chapter 42: The Awakening

The crimson moon hung low in the sky, casting long, unnatural shadows across the courtyard. Lin Fan stood motionless, his breath forming small clouds in the freezing air. The Qi swirling around him was chaotic, untamed, a stark contrast to his usually calm demeanor.

He had spent three years as the "trash" of the Lin family. Three years of enduring their sneers, their insults, their beatings. But tonight... tonight felt different. The ancient jade pendant his mother had left him was glowing with a faint, pulsing light against his chest.

"Is this it?" he murmured, clutching the pendant. The cold stone was now warm to the touch. A voice, ancient and resonant, echoed in his mind.

"The Bloodline of the Heavenly Sovereign cannot remain dormant forever. Awaken, my descendant."

Suddenly, a surge of power ripped through his meridians. It was agonizing, like molten iron flowing through his veins, but Lin Fan didn't scream. He gritted his teeth, a fierce light burning in his eyes. The age of humiliation was over.
